# Live campaign authorization checklist

- [ ] Program policy or engagement terms accepted and recorded privately
- [ ] Written authorization and agreement reference verified
- [ ] MNDA or confidentiality obligation confirmed when required
- [ ] Rules of engagement reviewed and confirmed
- [ ] Targets, services, APIs, accounts, and environments allowlisted
- [ ] Test window is active
- [ ] Categories and any state-changing actions explicitly allowed
- [ ] Forbidden actions and rate limits loaded
- [ ] Evidence storage, retention, redaction, reporting, and disclosure rules confirmed
- [ ] Emergency contact and stop conditions available
- [ ] Objective-specific human approvals recorded
- [ ] Operator passes `--operator-approved`

Any unchecked required item is a stop.
