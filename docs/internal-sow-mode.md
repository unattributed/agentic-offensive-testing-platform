# Internal SOW Mode

AOTP supports authorized enterprise AppSec, internal security, and red-team campaigns under a
statement of work or internal rules of engagement.

The operator may ingest an SOW from PDF, Markdown, saved HTML, or pasted text. The private profile
records production, staging, lab, and safe-exploit environments with distinct tool tiers,
credentials, windows, budgets, evidence rules, and approvals.

Internal ROE may authorize stronger testing than bug bounty mode. Source review can ingest local
repositories, and API review can ingest OpenAPI and GraphQL documentation. Source-derived hints do
not grant authority or add targets.

Targeted active testing, controlled input probing, exploitation validation, and replay require the
appropriate environment grant and high-friction approval unless the ROE explicitly pre-authorizes
the exact action. Remediation verification links new evidence to the original finding and approved
attack path.

Internal stakeholder packages include evidence-linked technical detail, remediation guidance, and
verification status. Sensitive proof is separated into an approval-gated annex.
